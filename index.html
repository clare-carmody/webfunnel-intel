The Non-Negotiable Floor — Present in All 6 Funnels

Empathy interstitial

A mid-quiz screen that validates without asking anything.
Sensa Q4: "It's natural to feel tired or overwhelmed — we've all been there."

Mid-funnel social proof

Inserted at ~65% completion, not on the landing page.
Paired: "91% of couples who talk about sex have better sex."

Expert credentialing

Science or authority cited inside the quiz itself.
Ahead Step 34: "Your plan is built on expert guidance."

Possessive "your" throughout

Every plan, profile, and result is named as owned by the user.
BetterMe: "Your attachment style personal growth plan."

5 Priority Recommendations — Click to Expand
1
Replace "Get Started" with Age-as-CTA
3 / 6 funnels ▼

3 of 6 funnels eliminate the dead "Get started" click entirely. The age buckets are the entry CTA — clicking your age begins the quiz. The user is inside the funnel before they've decided to take it.

"18–29 · 30–39 · 40–49 · 50+" — no CTA button anywhere on the page BetterMe entry screen
"18–24 · 25–34 · 35–44 · 45+" alongside "⏱ 2 min quiz" Paired entry screen
Melba action: Remove the "Take the quiz" button. Make "How long have you been together?" or age the opening interaction — the first tap starts the experience.
2
Insert Empathy Interstitial at the Moment of Disclosure
6 / 6 funnels ▼

Every funnel does this, but timing is everything. The interstitial must land after questions that confirm disconnection — not before, not randomly. This is the highest-leverage single screen in any funnel.

"We live in a fast-paced, sometimes difficult world. It's natural to feel tired or overwhelmed — we've all been there!" Sensa Q4 — immediately after confirming exhaustion
"You're not alone. Key ideas from leading scientists will help you heal." Ahead Step 10 — immediately after trauma multi-select
Melba action: After intimacy or connection scales confirm a gap: "Feeling disconnected is more common than you think. It doesn't have to stay that way — keep going and we'll build something just for the two of you."
3
Add Inline Answer Personalisation
2 / 6 funnels ▼

Only BetterMe and Ahead do this — and both are the most emotionally engaging funnels in the set. Custom feedback immediately after each answer makes users feel understood before they've downloaded anything. The single biggest differentiator available to Melba.

"Happy to hear that! Having parents who listened to your emotions gave you a huge advantage." Ahead Step 8 — positive answer on emotional expression scale
"Your caring nature is beautiful. Putting others first shows compassion, but you deserve care too." Ahead Step 16 — answer on boundaries scale
"We admire your sense of security, shine on!" BetterMe Phase 2 — adaptive interstitial after help-seeking questions
Melba action: After intimacy openness scale: "That kind of honesty is rare — and it's exactly what builds deeper connection." After low satisfaction: "It takes courage to name that. You're already doing the work."
4
Show the Result Before the Paywall
5 / 6 funnels ▼

5 of 6 funnels reveal something meaningful — an attachment style, a co-created plan, a named result — before asking for payment. Ahead is the only outlier, compensating with a hard countdown timer. Melba's positioning suits the reveal approach far better.

"Your attachment style: Anxiously attached" + plan preview shown before any pricing BetterMe — result reveal before paywall
"Creating your relationship plan" loader → plan summary shown → account creation → paywall Paired — full result-reveal-then-gate sequence
"Unlock Melt" — co-created story shown as waiting, locked behind payment Mr Arthur — result as the locked object
Melba action: "Here is the intimacy profile we built for you and your partner." Show a partially revealed plan. CTA: "Unlock your couple's plan" — not "Subscribe."
5
Rewrite the Paywall as "Unlock", Not "Subscribe"
2 / 6 funnels ▼

"Unlock" implies the content already belongs to the user and is currently withheld. "Subscribe" frames it as a new commercial transaction. Small word, large psychology — the difference between retrieving something yours and buying something foreign.

"Unlock Melt" — the user's co-created story is the locked object Mr Arthur paywall CTA
"51% discount reserved for: [9:57 countdown]" — "reserved for" implies prior ownership Ahead paywall header
Melba action: Change primary CTA from "Start your subscription" to "Unlock your couple's plan." Add: "Reserved for the next 10 minutes" beneath the pricing tiers to introduce scarcity.
Quick Wins — Wording Changes Only, No Structural Work
Wording to steal Source Apply in Melba as…
"Thanks for your honesty — we're almost there" Paired, pre-paywall interstitial Add immediately before the pricing screen: "You've shared something most couples never say out loud."
"I feel like we're going to break up soon" (1st-person option) BetterMe Q7 satisfaction scale Rewrite all answer options as complete 1st-person sentences — not frequency labels (Never / Sometimes / Often).
"So what brings you here?" (conversational "So") Paired Q7, Phase 3 Open the motivation question with "So…" to signal active listening rather than form-filling.
